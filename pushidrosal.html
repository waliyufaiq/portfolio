Collaborated cross-functionally with Navy personnel, demonstrating ability to communicate complex data for applied outcomes.
  • From the internship process, I developed a model of water elevation and current velocity in the Mahakam River in 2022, aimed at assessing potential flood risks, and also published an article for PUSHIDROSAL related to this work.
